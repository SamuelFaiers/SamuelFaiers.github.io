Subterranean Escape

  • Video Game Design
  • UI Design
  • 3D Modelling

For one of the modules within my degree required me to design and develop a video game in a team with two others. We decided on an adventure game that required the player to find keys in order to escape a collapsing cave before time runs out. My task was to design and develop the user interface of said video game. The desired genre of the game was that of fantasy, meaning the visual design of the UI had to reflect this. As with a website, the UI clearly presented information to the player with clear calls to action in the form of buttons.
