Chemical Energy Tour

This project was created at the request of my father- who desired an animation that would assist in teaching hs chemistry students about measures of energy. He specifically wanted it to be a parody of the Beatles album 'Magical Mystery Tour' featuring a bus similar to that featured in the music video.

It was ultimately decided that this would be a looping animation, featuring imagery relevant to the subject in the background. The idea behind this is that long term memory is encoded visually, therefore the use of images would reinforce the student's undertanding of the subject.
