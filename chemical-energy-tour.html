Chemical Energy Tour

  • Animation
  • Motion Graphics
  • Illustration

This project was created at the request of my father, who desired an animation that would assist in teaching chemistry students about measures of energy. He specifically wanted it to be a parody of the Beatles album ‘Magical Mystery Tour’ featuring a bus similar to that featured in the music video. It was ultimately decided that this would be a looping animation, featuring imagery relevant to the subject in the background to reinforce learning.
